Overview

Purpose

Zara Travel is a website dedicated to sharing the culture and beauty of the Madagascar island.
This website is for all people who love to travel and discover new cultures.
You will find here some beautiful and unique landscapes that make this island one of the most popular destinations for tourists.
Moreover, all the cooking lovers will find their happiness here, because there is a section containing various simple Malagasy recipes to reproduce to allow you to try new things.
Also, we propose you in our "Learn" section to learn some Malagasy sentences for your future trip or simply to increase your knowledge.
Thus, through Zara Travel, we will make you discover virtually one of the biggest island in the world so that you can relax and dream wherever you are.

Audience

This site is for all people who like to discover new cultures and travel: young people, adults, seniors, married, single, etc.
We do not discriminate on the basis of religion, gender or sexuality.
You can also consult the content of this site through different types of equipment: phone, computer, tablet, connected watch, etc.

Content

Home page

Madagascar, also called the "Big Island", is a country that offers multiple treasures. Come and discover a rich and unique culture because this country is part of the countries that have preserved their craft and gastronomic traditions. Moreover, it is the country of rare species with lemurs, fishing eagles, black crocodiles, whales, turtles, etc.

Images for the Home page

Pictures

Miles of white sand beaches along the coast, lagoons as beautiful as each other, palm trees and coconut palms that remind us that we are in the tropics, thousands of species of marine fauna, breathtaking seascapes, and above all a very mild climate: this is what you will discover in Madagascar, an assured change of scenery.
